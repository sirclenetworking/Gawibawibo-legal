Delete Your Account & Data

This page explains how to request deletion of your Gawibawibo account and the data associated with it.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@gawibawibo.com
  2. Use the subject line "Delete my account"
  3. Send it from the same email address associated with your Google or Apple sign-in, so we can verify the request
  4. We'll confirm your request by email and process it within 30 days

What gets deleted

Data typeDeleted?
Profile (username, avatar, email)Deleted
Match history & leaderboard statsDeleted
Account sign-in link (Google / Apple)Deleted
Aggregated, de-identified gameplay analyticsRetained (not linked to you)

De-identified analytics are kept only in aggregate form (e.g. "how many matches were played today") and can no longer be tied back to your account once deletion is complete.

Partial data deletion

If you'd like specific data deleted without deleting your entire account, mention this in your email and describe what you'd like removed (for example, your profile photo or match history) — we'll process what we can without removing your ability to sign in.
