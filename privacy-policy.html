1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Sircle Networking Pvt Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Gawibawibo mobile application (the "App"). Gawibawibo is a fan-made rock-paper-scissors (가위바위보) game with a BTS-inspired ARMY fan mode. Using the App means you're agreeing to how we handle your information as described below.

Gawibawibo is an independent, non-commercial fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by BTS, HYBE Corporation, or BIGHIT MUSIC. Fan references (e.g. "ARMY," "BTS mode") are used solely for non-commercial fan-tribute purposes. All third-party trademarks belong to their respective owners.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

If you sign in with Google or Apple, we (via our authentication provider, Supabase) receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier. Apple Sign-In may allow you to hide your real email using Apple's private relay.

Profile information

  • Username you choose
  • Profile picture — either a default avatar or a community fan-art image you select
  • Whether you've identified yourself as "ARMY" in your profile

Fan art submissions

If you submit fan art, we collect the image file and any title/caption you provide. Submitted art is reviewed (including automated moderation via Microsoft Azure AI content moderation) before being made available for other users to select as profile pictures. Please only submit original artwork you created yourself.

Gameplay data

  • Match results, rounds played, wins/losses/draws
  • Matchmaking and player-vs-player (PvP) match data, including your opponent's match outcomes
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Device platform (iOS/Android) and app version

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymized/pseudonymized usage events (e.g. app opened, round played, match completed, share button tapped) to help us understand how the App is used and to improve it.

Device permissions

The App may request certain device permissions (such as photo library access, for uploading a profile picture or fan art, and in some builds, microphone access for potential future features). We only access these when required for the relevant feature, and you can decline or revoke permissions at any time in your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Creating and managing your account and profile
  • Running core gameplay — AI matches, PvP matchmaking, leaderboards
  • Moderating and displaying fan art submitted by the community
  • Understanding how the App is actually used, so we can fix what's broken and improve what isn't
  • Responding to support requests, if you reach out
  • Catching and addressing bugs, fraud, or abuse

We do not sell your personal information — not now, not later.

4. How We Share Information

We're not in the business of sharing your data around. The only places it goes are the providers that keep the App running:

5. Data Storage & Retention

Your data lives on Supabase's cloud infrastructure. We hold onto account and gameplay data for as long as your account stays active. Delete your account — or just ask us to (details below) — and we'll delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period, unless we're required to keep it for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access & correction: You can view and edit your username and profile picture within the App.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see below).
  • Sign-out: You can sign out of the App at any time from the profile menu.
  • Permissions: You can revoke photo library or other device permissions in your device settings.

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR (European Economic Area) or CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Security

9. International Users
