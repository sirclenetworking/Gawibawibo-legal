Tap "Continue with Google" or "Continue with Apple" on the sign in screen. There's also a "Tap to play" option if you'd rather jump in as a guest first, you can always sign in properly later to save your progress and appear on the leaderboard.

Tap Play to enter matchmaking. You'll be paired with a real opponent within seconds. If nobody's around, a bot steps in so you're never stuck waiting, matches are best of 3 rounds either way.

The leaderboard ranks the Top 100 players by PvP wins. Every match you win against a real opponent counts toward your rank, so the more you play and win, the higher you climb.
